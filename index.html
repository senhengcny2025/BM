In 2013, Senheng shifted its business direction towards new platforms, embracing the power of digital marketing and social media. The BM Area reflects this transformation with a unique decoration concept that blends tradition and modernity. The 福字 displayed here is formed from hand-written wishes contributed by the BM team, symbolizing unity in the shape of a heart. This area also features a Facebook-style template, where colleagues’ New Year wishes are shared, creating a virtual space for connection and celebration.
